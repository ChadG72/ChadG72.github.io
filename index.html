Step 1: Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.

Step 2: Beat egg, milk and oil in medium bowl with whisk until blended. Stir in flour mixture.

Step 1: Cream butter, peanut butter and both sugars together.

Step 2: Add eggs, one at a time, beating well.

Step 3: Add baking soda, powder and vanilla.

Step 4: Stir in flour.

Step 5:Roll into balls, roll in sugar. Flatten cookies, using a fork, in a criss-cross pattern.
